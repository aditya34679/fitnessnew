Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items, with the exception of clearance products and certain accessories.

2. Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free shipping on all orders over $100 within the U.S. International shipping rates may vary.

3. How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options are available during checkout.

4. Do you offer fitness coaching services?

Yes, we offer personalized coaching services, including virtual sessions. For more information, visit our "Coaching" page.

5. Can I cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placing them. After that, the order is processed for shipment and cannot be canceled.
